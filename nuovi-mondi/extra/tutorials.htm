Art tutorials

These tutorials take you through the artists' process in creating their art. Each tutorial is unique, tailored to the artist's style and preferences.

Maybe you're interested in improving those wave doodles of yours!? Well check out the hugely popular "How to draw a wave" tutorial by surfing comic strip illustrator Bob Penuelas.

Or perhaps you're more interested in the process of how a painting comes together? In that case, check out how ex-Marvel & Disney artist Damian Fulton created his "An Eye for an Eye" painting. Or see how the event artwork for the "2007 Roxy Jam France" and "2011 Reef Hawaiian Pro" were created.
