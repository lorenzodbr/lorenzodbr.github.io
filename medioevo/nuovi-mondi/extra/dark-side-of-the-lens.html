Dark Side of The Lens

We highly recommend you check out a really beautiful and inspiring short film by Astray Films and COTW featured surf photographer Mickey Smith titled "Dark Side of The Lens". This is the first in a new series called "Short Stories" by Relentless Energy.

Watch this great, inspiring short documentary below…

Update June 2012: We’re delighted to announce that Dark Side of the Lens won the Action Sports category of the 2012 Vimeo Festival Awards!
