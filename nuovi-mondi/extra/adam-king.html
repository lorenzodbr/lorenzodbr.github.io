Adam King

Scroll

Photographer — Amelia Island, Florida

I love photography. From my early memories getting to handle my father’s old 35mm Canon AE-1, to charging into the ocean hoping to catch the magical morning light shining through backlit waves, capturing the moment the curtain opens on a band’s big night, working together on a special portrait session, and most dear to my heart, capturing my family and freezing every special moment I can, to be enjoyed for years to come. I’m based on Amelia Island, Florida, just north of Jacksonville. I am a father and husband, with a passion for capturing our love of the ocean, the connection we have with it, and that power and beauty that we must appreciate.
