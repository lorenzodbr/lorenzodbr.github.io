Surfploytation by Damian Fulton

Artist, Damian Fulton is fascinated by the dichotomy and duality of Los Angeles beach culture. The proximity of one of the world’s most diverse and congested cities to the Pacific coast provides fertile material for Damian’s dark take on the California dream. Perhaps it’s in the water.

Surfploytation continues Damian Fulton’s twisted ride down the Pacific Coast Highway where Surf City is way more Gotham than groovy. Car chases, girls with guns, noir nostalgia, a visual narrative that reads like conceptual art for a fictional Surfploytation movie. Have this new work amped up to 11.

Before focusing on his own vision, Damian Fulton helped Disneyland, Marvel Productions, and various magazines find theirs.

Curated by Andrew Couldwell on Oct 25, 2011
