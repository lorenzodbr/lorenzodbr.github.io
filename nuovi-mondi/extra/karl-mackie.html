Karl Mackie

Scroll

Photographer — Cornwall, England

Stories from the road less travelled. Karl is a surfer, photographer, craftsman and owner of Mackie, an award-winning creative branding agency based in Cornwall. With a background in design and branding, his passion lies in creating engaging content. Documenting lifestyles and travel, often amongst the coastal and surfing community has garnered Karl a reputation as someone who can capture the moments between the moments, these are often where the true story lies and that comes through in his imagery. Karl lives in Newquay, Cornwall with his family and faithful hound, Willow. A lifelong surfer, he can often be found at his local break or in his backyard shaping bay tinkering with surfboards.
