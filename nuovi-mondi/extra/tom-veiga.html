Tom Veiga

Scroll

Artist — Curitiba, Brazil

Tom Veiga was born in Curitiba, in the southern region of Brazil. Before becoming an artist he worked as an Art Director in several design agencies. His passion for the sea has awakened his vocation for art. His art is the union of his work as a designer and his passion for the sea, inspired by the sensations and feelings of the wind of the sea, the heat of the sun and the movement of the waves. His art has a very personal language, characterized by many colors, few strokes and much movement. With this style the artist seeks to reflect his vision of the waves, reflecting the differences they have around the world. Tom Veiga has a very free creative process for creating his arts. Today, besides the digital works he also has the painted versions of them.
