Rick Piper

Scroll

Artist — Cocoa Beach, Florida

Born in Florida, Rick Piper is the son of a preacher and avid fisherman. Fascinated by moving water, waves appear as one of the main themes in his diverse art work. Known for his unique perspectives and vibrant color, Rick’s work has become compositional in nature over time. Springing forth from his prodigious imagination, rooted more in feeling and memory than the representation of any particular place or time. Prolific and varied, Rick’s work is widely collected and in high demand, originals being viewed (and usually sold) almost immediately upon completion through his e-mail list available on his website.
