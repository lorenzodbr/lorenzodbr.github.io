Jules Ombak

Scroll

Artist — Sochi, Russia

My name is Yulia Krivosheeva (aka Jules Ombak). I love art and trying to capture significant moments with painting and photography. Deep in love with the ocean, always seeking for adventures, and trying to make life a little more beautiful. Art is my favourite thing to do, but I never thought of making money through it, so I studied finance at university. Upon graduating I studied interior design, which is now my main occupation. After visiting Bali for the first time, in 2012, I felt so excited and impressed that I decided to return to art and share my impressions with the world! I was so amazed by the works of Indonesian artists, I decided to open a small Indo art gallery. In my free time I love to travel, take photos and write about people I meet and places I visit. And, of course, paint and share my passion with you!
